Child Safety Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Our Commitment

IndoChat is absolutely committed to child safety. We have a zero-tolerance policy toward any content or behavior that exploits, harms, or endangers minors in any way.

Important: IndoChat is strictly an 18+ platform. Minors are prohibited from using this App.

2. Age Restriction

IndoChat is designed exclusively for adults aged 18 and above. We enforce this through:

  • Age verification during registration
  • Terms of Service requiring users to confirm they are 18+
  • Immediate account termination if a minor is detected
  • Reporting mechanisms for suspected underage users

3. Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited on IndoChat:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Any sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming behavior targeting minors
  • Any content that exploits, abuses, or endangers children
  • Sharing of minors' personal information

Violation of this policy will result in immediate account termination and reporting to law enforcement authorities.

4. Detection & Prevention

We take active measures to prevent child exploitation:

  • Admin monitoring tools to detect suspicious behavior
  • User reporting system for inappropriate content
  • Immediate ban of users violating child safety policies
  • Cooperation with law enforcement when required

5. Reporting Mechanism

If you encounter any content or behavior that you believe endangers a child:

  • Report immediately using the in-app report button
  • Email us at: safety@indochat.app
  • Contact your local law enforcement
  • Report to NCMEC (National Center for Missing & Exploited Children): www.cybertipline.org

All reports are taken seriously and investigated promptly.

6. Law Enforcement Cooperation

IndoChat fully cooperates with law enforcement agencies in cases involving child safety. We will:

  • Preserve and provide user data when legally required
  • Proactively report CSAM to appropriate authorities
  • Assist in investigations involving child exploitation

7. Data Deletion for Minors

If we discover that a minor has created an account:

  • The account will be immediately terminated
  • All associated data will be permanently deleted
  • The incident will be logged for safety purposes
