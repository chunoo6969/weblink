Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

IndoChat ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

We believe in minimal data collection. We only collect what is necessary to provide our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Username, gender (optional), and profile preferences
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Data: Chat session duration, connection timestamps
  • Firebase Authentication: Anonymous or email-based authentication data

We do NOT collect:

  • Your real name or address
  • Payment information
  • Chat message content (messages are not stored on our servers)
  • Precise GPS location

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Match you with other users based on your preferences
  • Maintain and improve the App
  • Detect and prevent abuse or violations
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Chat messages are transmitted in real-time and are NOT permanently stored on our servers. Once a chat session ends, messages are gone.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and database storage
  • Google Firebase Storage: Media file storage (images, videos)
  • Render.com: Server hosting

Each of these services has their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users
  • With service providers who assist in operating our App

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@indochat.app

8. Children's Privacy

IndoChat is strictly for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover that a minor has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete it.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

Email: privacy@indochat.app
